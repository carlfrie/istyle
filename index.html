My Story

I began DJing in 2009 while a student at Silpakorn University. I taught myself to scratch and mix as well.

Currently, I am a resident DJ at Hyde & Seek Peek-a-boo, located in CentralWorld. I play a blend of styles tailor made for patrons to enjoy.

In July of 2016, I was honored to be one of 6 finalists for Redbull Thre3style. Performing my scratch routine in front of a full house was a dream come true.

In 2015, I played several slots at the opening of DJ Land in Fukuoka, Japan. This was a 48-hour non-stop event featuring 20 international DJs.

From 2013 - 2014 I co-hosted Beat Kontrol with my husband at Bootleg DJ Cafe in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. This was a series of underground parties focusing on Deep House & Techno. Through Beat Kontrol I made many great friends and developed my mixing style.

I'm also active in supporting the scratch community in Bangkok by sharing scratch skills & techniques as well as supplying equipment.
